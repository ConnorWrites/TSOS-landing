      <p class="m">This book tells the story of a boy discovering a world lying just beyond the edge of perception. His insatiable curiosity leads him on his journey, and takes him on a fantastic adventure where he discovers the wonders of the realm in much the same way one discovers the ground upon falling from a tree.

William is a boy who loves stories. Stories fill his dreams, spark his imagination, and fuel his creativity, yet he’s always wondered how it all works and how it’s connected.

One night, William jolts up as loud, crashing thunder of a strange storm rages over the town. He rushes to close his window, but sees a mysterious figure standing outside in the dark as lightning dances through the night sky. Terror grips him as it turns to face him, and he stands frozen in the otherworldly glow of the eyes of the creature.</p>

Its regular customers, known as Gumplegruffs, work at the factory where the story scrolls are made, and whispers of stories that threaten to upset or even destroy the natural balance of the universe, often make their way into the beloved pub, now known as the Tavern of Forbidden Tales.

All stories on this page come from that peculiar tavern.</p>
